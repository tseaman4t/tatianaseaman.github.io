Tatiana Seaman - English Tango

A film about where truth lives — mind vs body.

Feature Film

ENGLISH TANGO

An English tango teacher struggling to save her failing school is forced to confront buried desire and war trauma when a mysterious tango master disrupts a fragile love triangle.

Statement

I am interested in where truth resides when language fails.

In English Tango, the body remembers what the mind suppresses. Movement reveals what cannot be spoken — and sometimes what should not be.

The film does not explain. It allows behaviour to surface meaning.

Control, restraint, and form begin to fracture until something more honest takes their place.
